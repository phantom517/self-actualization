The term ‘Vedas’ is taken from a sanskrit word ‘Vid’ means knowledge or to know. The Vedas contain a vast amount of information about life in India at various times and places. [UPSC Notes]

The Nature of God in Hinduism Hinduism, one of the world's oldest religions, offers a profound and multifaceted understanding of the nature of God. At the heart of Hindu belief systems lies a diverse and expansive perception of the divine that encapsulates various deities, philosophical concepts, and spiritual principles. The depiction of God in Hinduism is dynamic and encompasses both monotheistic and polytheistic elements, allowing for a comprehensive exploration of the divine nature.
